KJ Osborn

KJ Osborn built his game through discipline, route precision, and relentless effort. As an NFL wide receiver, he has modeled the kind of professional preparation young athletes can follow.

At camp, KJ has shared receiving fundamentals, footwork details, and the mindset needed to compete at every level. His message to campers is clear: consistency and work ethic create opportunity.
