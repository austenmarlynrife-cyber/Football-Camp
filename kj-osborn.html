KJ Osborn

Kendrick Ladell "K.J." Osborn Jr. is an NFL wide receiver who played college football at Buffalo (2015 to 2018) and Miami (FL) (2019) before entering the draft.

Drafted by Minnesota in the 2020 NFL Draft (Round 5, Pick 176), Osborn went on to play for the Vikings and later had stints with New England and Washington, with Atlanta as a practice squad stop in 2025.

Notable achievements include his 2021 breakout season with 655 receiving yards and 7 touchdowns and key production during Minnesota's historic 2022 comeback win over Indianapolis.

Career receiving stats through 2025: 165 receptions, 1,902 receiving yards, and 16 receiving touchdowns.
