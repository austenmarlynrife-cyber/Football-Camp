Harrison Phillips

Harrison Phillips is an NFL nose tackle who played college football at Stanford (2014 to 2017), where he earned First-Team All-Pac-12 honors in 2017.

He was selected by Buffalo in the 2018 NFL Draft (Round 3, Pick 96) and later played for Minnesota before being traded to the New York Jets in 2025.

A notable pro milestone was his 2023 season with a career-high 92 tackles while anchoring Minnesota's defensive front.

Career defensive stats through 2025: 374 combined tackles and 8.5 sacks.
