Registration

Redeye River Football Camp is a FREE camp thanks to the generous donations of our sponsors. Space is limited, and registration is first come, first serve.

Register Now

Who Can Register

  • Open to boys and girls entering Kindergarten through 6th grade.
  • Typical age range is about 5-12 years old (based on grade level).
  • Pre-registration is required to guarantee a camp t-shirt.

Camp Dates, Times, and Locations

  • July 15: Menahga High School football field, 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM (arrive early to finish registration).
  • July 16: Sebeka High School football field, 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM.
  • July 17: Sebeka High School football field, 3:00 PM - 6:00 PM.
  • You can choose Day 1, Day 2, and/or Day 3 in the registration form.
