John Randle

John Randle rose from an undrafted start to become one of the fiercest and most respected defensive linemen in football history. His career reflected grit, toughness, and relentless pursuit.

As a Hall of Famer, his impact extends beyond statistics. Young players learn from his story that mindset and motor can separate a good athlete from a great one.
