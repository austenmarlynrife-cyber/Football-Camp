Our Mission

More Than A Football Camp

Red Eye River Football Camp exists to invest in young athletes across northern Minnesota, giving every kid access to elite coaching, real mentorship, and a team-first environment, completely free of charge.

Building Community Togetherness

Football has a unique power to bring people together, and that's exactly what this camp is built around. Red Eye River Football Camp is more than a skills clinic. It's a gathering point for families, coaches, and athletes from across the region to come together, compete, encourage one another, and grow as a community.

We believe the bonds formed on the football field carry far beyond it. The friendships made here, the encouragement shared between teammates, and the energy of hundreds of people coming together for the love of the game, that's what this camp is really about.

Growing Football in Northern Minnesota

Great football talent doesn't only come from the big cities. Northern Minnesota is full of passionate, hardworking young athletes who deserve access to high-level instruction, and that's exactly what Red Eye River Football Camp provides.

By bringing NFL-level coaching and training directly to the communities of Menahga and Sebeka, we're investing in the future of football in this region. Our goal is to help players develop the technical skills and mental edge they need to compete at the next level, and to show every camper that where you're from doesn't limit how far you can go.

  • Position-focused instruction and drill stations
  • Speed, agility, and footwork development
  • Teamwork and confidence-building leadership sessions
  • Pro-level perspective from NFL athletes and experienced coaches

Free To Every Camper, Made Possible By Our Sponsors

From day one, our commitment has been simple: no camper should miss out because of cost. Red Eye River Football Camp is entirely free for every participant, and that's only possible because of the incredible generosity of our sponsors.

Local businesses, community partners, and supporters who believe in these kids make every jersey and every moment of this camp possible. When you sponsor Red Eye River Football Camp, you're not just putting your name on a banner, you're giving a kid an experience they'll never forget.
