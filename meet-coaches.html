Additional Coaches

Tanner Burgau

Tanner is focused on play design and helping campers understand how to execute smart, team-first concepts.

  • Teaches core play design and formation alignment.
  • Breaks down routes, spacing, and timing concepts.
  • Shows athletes how to communicate within each play call.

Tyler Holmquist

Tyler is focused on games and teamwork, creating competitive sessions that build chemistry and communication.

  • Leads small-sided competitive games and challenge stations.
  • Reinforces communication, trust, and shared responsibility.
  • Builds team habits that transfer to game situations.

Chris Arthur

Chris helps campers compete with confidence while keeping sessions positive, focused, and fun.

  • Leads competitive skill games and reps.
  • Teaches communication on and off the field.
  • Builds leadership habits for game situations.

Jake Anderson

Jake is focused on offense and technique, helping players execute with confidence and precision.

  • Teaches offensive footwork, ball skills, and timing.
  • Breaks down reads, progressions, and decision-making.
  • Reinforces clean fundamentals at game speed.

Seth Zins

Seth is focused on defense and technique, teaching disciplined fundamentals on every rep.

  • Coaches defensive stance, pursuit angles, and leverage.
  • Emphasizes tackling form and technique-first execution.
  • Builds defensive communication and assignment discipline.
