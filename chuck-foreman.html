Chuck Foreman

Walter Eugene "Chuck" Foreman starred at Miami (FL) before being selected by the Minnesota Vikings in the 1973 NFL Draft (Round 1, Pick 12).

He became one of the NFL's most dynamic dual-threat running backs, later also playing for New England, and helped lead Minnesota to three Super Bowl appearances.

Notable accolades include NFL Offensive Rookie of the Year (1973), 5 Pro Bowls, 2 First-Team All-Pro honors, and NFL receptions leader in 1975.

Career stats: 5,950 rushing yards, 53 rushing touchdowns, 350 receptions, and 3,156 receiving yards.
